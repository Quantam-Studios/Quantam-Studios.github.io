John Conway's Game of Life Recreation

SIMULATOR

A complete recreation of the famous cellular automata "Game of Life" by John Conway. Click on the cells you want to be alive or click live ones to make them dead. Then press "Space" and watch your creation come to life. Press "0" to end the current simulation and begin placing new cells.
